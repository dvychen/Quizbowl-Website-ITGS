What is Quizbowl?

Quizbowl is a fun and exciting test of knowledge and wits for everyone! Each round, players are divided into two teams and race to answer trivia questions the fastest. Whoever answers the most comes out on top to win! It’s like Jeopardy, but more exciting and fast-paced!

Quizbowl is comprised of many topics, including literature, science, fine arts, history, geography, RMP (religion, mythology, philosophy), pop culture, social science, and current events. There’s a topic for all!

Quizbowl usually attends a total of four regional tournaments, including the Ottawa Novice Bowl (for beginners), the regional Quiz Bowl, Reach for the Top (quick format quizbowl), and the regional History Bowl (a competition focusing solely on history).
